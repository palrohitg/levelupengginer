

# Level Up Engineers – Website Plan

## Overview
A clean, professional website for Level Up Engineers to showcase courses, build trust with students, and capture leads for enrollment. The logo will be embedded on the site with a blue-accented, light-themed design.

---

## Pages & Sections

### 1. Home Page (Landing Page)
- **Navbar**: Logo (Level Up Engineers), navigation links (Home, Courses, About, Contact), and a prominent "Book a Free Session" CTA button
- **Hero Section**: Bold headline like "Crack Your Dream Tech Job", subheadline about mentorship from FAANG engineers, CTA buttons ("Explore Courses" and "Talk to a Counselor"), and key stats (e.g., students placed, mentor companies, success rate)
- **Courses Overview**: Cards for all 4 courses (Interview Prep, Backend, DevOps+SRE, Full Stack) with duration, highlights, and "Learn More" buttons linking to individual course pages
- **Why Level Up Engineers**: Key differentiators – 1:1 mentorship, resume review, referrals to top companies, instructors from Zomato/Google/Amazon/Microsoft etc.
- **Instructor Showcase**: Grid of instructor cards with name, company logo/name, and role (placeholder data for now)
- **Testimonials/Success Stories**: Student testimonial cards (placeholder content)
- **Lead Capture CTA Banner**: "Ready to Level Up?" section with a quick enrollment form or button to contact page
- **Footer**: Logo, quick links, social media links, contact info

### 2. Individual Course Pages (4 pages)
Each course gets a dedicated page with:
- Course title, duration, and overview
- Curriculum/syllabus breakdown (modules/topics)
- Key highlights (mentorship, referrals, resume review, etc.)
- "Talk to a Counselor" CTA (no pricing shown)
- Lead capture form at the bottom

### 3. About Page
- Company mission and story
- Why Level Up Engineers exists
- Team/instructor highlights
- Company values (mentorship-first, placement-focused, etc.)

### 4. Contact Page
- Lead capture form (Name, Email, Phone, Course Interest dropdown, Message)
- Contact details (email, phone if applicable)
- Optional: Embedded map or office location

---

## Lead Capture Form
A form that collects: **Name, Email, Phone Number, Course of Interest** (dropdown), and optional **Message**. On submission, a success toast notification confirms the inquiry was received. Initially, form data will be stored locally (no backend) – a backend (Lovable Cloud) can be added later to store leads in a database.

---

## Design Direction
- **Light, clean background** with blue accents matching the logo
- Professional typography, generous whitespace
- Subtle hover animations on cards and buttons
- Mobile-responsive throughout
- Trust-building elements: company logos of instructors' employers, stats, testimonials
